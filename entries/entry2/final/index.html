Tam Dao is a beautifully crafted diptyque perfume with the velvety scent of sandalwood.
With its thoughtfully selected elements, this scent captures the essence of Vietnam's Tam Dao mountains, where old sandalwood trees bloom.
The rosewood adds a gentle sweetness and an aroma of spice to the sandalwood's creamy richness as the smell grows smoothly.
Tam Dao is a unisex fragrance that reflects the natural beauty of sandalwood.
FRAGRANCE NOTES

SANDALWOOD - It captures the rich, woody, and creamy aroma of the precious Mysore sandalwood.
CYPRESS - Cypress gives the piece a delightful green and slightly aromatic feel, which implies the icy, wooded mountains of Tam Dao.
ROSEWOOD - It adds a gentle sweetness and an air of spice to the aroma, adding its richness and depth.
